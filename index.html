Ankith Girish

Hi, I’m Ankith Girish, a high school senior passionate about engineering, aviation, and photography.

I’ve been into aviation since I was a kid—folding paper planes, flying drones, and just being obsessed with how things fly. I recently published a book on AP Physics 1—it’s a study guide that helps students understand the core concepts while giving them plenty of practice to really lock it in.

I’m also working on a spokeless wheelchair project that’s in the process of getting patented. It’s something I’ve poured a lot of time into, and I’m excited about the impact it could have.

Outside the classroom, I like using my creative side—mixing visual effects with theatre and drama to create powerful visuals.

I’m applying to study mechanical engineering in college, and someday, I want to build tech that makes the world smarter, faster, more beautiful, and sustainable.
