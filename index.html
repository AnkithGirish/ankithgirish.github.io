Redesigning Mobility

A novel spokeless wheelchair that reduces injuries related to spokes and improves quality of life for users. Designed with a hubless wheel system and integrated features for daily convenience and safety.

This novel spokeless wheelchair that reduces injuries related to spokes. The wheelchair has a hubless wheel system; the wheels are supported only by the outer tire and rotate around a stationary inner rim. This eliminates spoke-related hazards like entanglement and other injuries. The wheelchair also features quality-of-life improvements like storage bins, tray table, along with an internal folding mechanism for easy transport and storage. It is designed to improve stability, mobility, and user comfort. To enhance stability, the wheels are tilted outward at approximately 10 degrees, effectively lowering the center of mass. This improves stability at high speeds, such as going down a ramp. It also optimizes propulsion, letting users move with minimal energy. A brake is placed near the wheel and activated by moving the handle forward. Push handles, a detachable tray table, and storage bins within the hollow wheel provide convenience—without compromising the wheelchair’s minimalist, modern design.
